🚂 Why this is special: the Øresund Bridge train
After landing in Copenhagen, you take the SJ X2000 train from Copenhagen Central to Stockholm Central — a ~5h journey that crosses the famous Øresund Bridge and tunnel between Denmark and Sweden. It's one of Scandinavia's great rail journeys: the bridge emerges from a tunnel in the sea, reveals open water, then connects to mainland Sweden through Malmö. For a train enthusiast this is a genuine experience, not just a transit leg.
Trains run every ~2 hours. From ~€17 advance (SJ.se). Book at 90 days out for the best fares.

⛴ Why Eckerölinjen is worth it: the M/S Eckerö experience
The M/S Eckerö is an intimate ship with a completely different vibe from the giant Viking Line vessels. The 2h crossing goes through the outer Åland archipelago — sea views, skerries, proper island ferry atmosphere. There's a live band in Bar Klubb Kuling, a tax-free shop, and an archipelago buffet (optional, ~€27/pp). Unlike the 7h Viking Line overnight ships, this is a short, bright morning crossing that arrives in daylight. The ferry terminal (Berghamn) has a public bus to Mariehamn in Bussplan that connects with every arrival — pre-purchase the bus ticket when booking the ferry.
Timetable from Grisslehamn: 10:00 / 15:00 / 20:00 daily. The 10:00 departure is the morning option. Journey takes 2h to Eckerö (local Åland time, which is EEST = Swedish time + 1h).
