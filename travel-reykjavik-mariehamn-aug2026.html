⚠️ Tight connection at Riga (RIX) — 40 min layover
Airport MCT: Riga Airport's published Minimum Connection Time for Air Baltic (their hub carrier) international → international is 40 minutes — this is the absolute floor, with zero buffer.
Carrier guarantee: Both legs are operated and sold by Air Baltic on a single booking, making this a protected connection. If the KEF→RIX leg is delayed and you miss the onward flight, Air Baltic is legally obligated to rebook you on the next available flight to Stockholm at no extra cost (EU Regulation 261/2004 also applies as KEF is an EU-adjacent departure).
The real risk: If you miss the connection, the next Air Baltic Stockholm flight is likely afternoon. You would miss the 16:00 Stockholm→Mariehamn ferry, and the next sailing wouldn't reach Mariehamn until 22:50 on 4 Aug — one day past your deadline.
Checked bags: Even if Air Baltic rebooks you onto a later flight, your checked bags may travel on a different flight and arrive separately. Bag-claim at ARN adds further time risk against the 16:00 ferry.
